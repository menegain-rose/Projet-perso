Glossary

Me too
It is a site where victims of rape, sexual assault, and sexual harassment. May anonymously post their testimonials.
The amazons
In Greek mythology, they were both warriors and feminists. Among the first women to wear pants ...
Excision
It is genital mutilation, which affects millions of women around the world. A tradition, a passage to become a woman.
Interview
Concerted meeting between two people who have to talk, to deal with a case.
Rite of passage
It marks the change of social status or sexuality of an individual, often at the social puberty and also at birth or menopause.
Rape
Sexual intercourse forced on a person without their consent.
Autobiography
Book in which the author is also the main character, which tells about his life or a moment in his life.
