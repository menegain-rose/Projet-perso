Simone veil

Simone Veil born July 13, 1927 and died June 30, 2017. She is a French magistrate and stateswoman. After studying law and political science, she entered the judiciary as a senior civil servant. In 1974, she became Minister of Health. She passed the law decriminalizing the use of voluntary termination of pregnancy (abortion), a law later called "Veil law".

Hélène Cixous

Hélène Cixous, she was born on June 5, 1937, she is a French writer and playwright.
Co-founder of the Center Universitaire de Vincennes, in 1974 she created the first center for women's studies in a European university.
Her essay Le Rire de la Méduse (1975) is considered a seminal work of modern feminism.

Waris Dirie

Waris Dirie is a woman born October 21, 1965 in Somalia.
When she was 13, she fled when her father decided to marry her to a 60-year-old man.
A modeling career began in 1987 on the cover of “Pirelli Calendar”.
In an interview Waris reveals excision. She wrote her first autobiographical book “Desert Flower” in 1998.
The fight against female circumcision began in 2002, speaking and when she decided to found the “WARIS DIRIE FOUNDATION”.
